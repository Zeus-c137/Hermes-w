Explore Hermes Features

Hermes bridges local mobile money with Ethereum. Mint and redeem UDX, send P2P, pay merchants, and swap to stablecoins — without blockchain complexity.

Core Hermes capabilities

  • Mobile Money ↔ Blockchain Bridge.

    Deposit via Airtel/MTN to mint UDX instantly, and redeem 1:1 back to mobile money whenever you need.

  • Peer-to-Peer Transfers.

    Send UDX or supported stablecoins instantly to friends, family, or businesses — borderless and fast.

  • Utility & Merchant Payments.

    Pay bills, airtime, and local merchants with UDX or stablecoins directly from Hermes.

  • Stablecoin Swaps & Virtual Card.

    Swap UDX ↔ USDT/USDC/DAI and activate a virtual card to spend globally online.

  • Developer & Business APIs.

    Integrate Hermes rails for payouts, collections, and treasury flows with simple APIs and webhooks.

  • Gasless UX

  • Global Access

  • Merchant Payments

  • Stablecoin Swaps

Why Hermes?

Not just another wallet. Hermes is a financial layer: mint UDX, pay, swap, and move funds gaslessly with mobile-first simplicity.

⚡ Gasless UX via meta-transactions.
