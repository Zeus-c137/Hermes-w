About Hermes

Hermes is the financial teleporter for Africa — a permissionless wallet that bridges mobile money and the blockchain with UDX, gasless UX, P2P transfers, merchant payments, swaps, and virtual cards.

Financial teleportation for everyone.

Product pillars
See what Hermes can do

Hermes connects local mobile money rails with Ethereum. Deposit to mint UDX (a digital UGX unit), send P2P instantly, pay merchants, swap to stablecoins, and spend online with a virtual card — without the blockchain learning curve.

We obsess over access, simplicity, and trust: access to borderless money, simple experiences that feel native, and verifiable transparency at every step.

Built by operators in the region, for the region. Hermes is designed to work in the real world — on any device, in any network condition, for anyone.

Built for real people and real money

Hermes began with a simple question: what if moving value was as familiar as sending an SMS, but settled on open rails?

We designed UDX to feel local and liquid, and layered gasless UX so everyday actions don’t require blockchain expertise. Since day one, we’ve shipped for low bandwidth, low friction, and high trust.
